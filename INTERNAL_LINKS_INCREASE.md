## Link Quality Standards

### ‚úÖ All Links Follow Best Practices:

1. **Relative Paths**
   - All links use relative paths (`/about`, `/contact`)
   - No hardcoded absolute URLs
   - SEO-friendly and maintainable

2. **Descriptive Anchor Text**
   - Clear, descriptive link text
   - No generic "click here" links
   - Contextually relevant

3. **Strategic Placement**
   - Links placed in relevant content sections
   - InternalLinks components at bottom of pages
   - Contextual links within content

4. **Canonical URLs**
   - All links resolve to www canonical URLs
   - No non-www URLs in internal links
   - Consistent URL structure
